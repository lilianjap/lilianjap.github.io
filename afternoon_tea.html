Afternoon Tea

For 2 years, me and my partner Cecilia de la Motte produced and hosted Afternoon Tea, existing both as a radioshow and podcast. The concept is based on getting to know guests in a more personal identifying way rather than typical displays that works in the public media-sphere.

Between the years 2014-2016 it hosted at Stockholm College Radio and the podcast produced as a part of Osqledaren, the official student union magazine at KTH. Guests included Per Sinding-Larsen, Kodjo Akolor, Kristian Luuk, Adam Tensta, XOV, Tussilago, Kumba, Brita Zackari, Azure Blue et al.
